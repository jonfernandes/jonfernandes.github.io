Can I get assistance for a connecting UK train journey?

If you're making a connection in the UK, just call either National Rail Enquiries (+44 (0)8457 48 49 50) or the individual train operator 24 hours before your journey. The system can book one-off journeys or a series of frequently made journeys. Once you're registered on the system, your details can be shared with other UK train companies.

If you're taking a taxi, most London black cabs have ramps, so they're accessible for wheelchair users.

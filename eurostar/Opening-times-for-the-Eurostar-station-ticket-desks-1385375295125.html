Opening times for the Eurostar station ticket desks.

Opening times for the Eurostar station ticket desks are as follows: St Pancras International Monday and Friday will open at 04:30 Tuesday, Wed and Thurs - will open at 0445 Saturday will open 04:30 Sunday will open at 06:45 (St Pancras Ticket Office will close shortly after the last Eurostar departure of the day) St Pancras International Travel Centre Monday to Saturday 08:30 to 20:00 Sunday 09:30 to 20:00 Ebbsfleet International 06:00 to 18:30 (Monday to Friday) 06:00 to 18:30 (Saturday) 07:30 to 18:30 (Sunday) The Ticket Desk closes at 18.30. After this time Eurostar Staff are situated in the Departures Lounge and periodically man the Concourse Area in Arrivals as passengers come off the platform. Ashford International The ticket office will be closed from 14:00 onwards (only re-opening between 16:00 - 16:45 for our only afternoon departure).

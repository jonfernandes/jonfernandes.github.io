What happens if I need to remove a team member from my business?

Once a team member is removed from a business, they will no longer be associated to your business account.

Any bookings they made prior to leaving the business (whether past or upcoming) will remain associated and visible in your administrator company ‘Bookings’ tab (for management and reconciliation purposes).

Any new bookings they make after leaving the business will not appear in your company account. These will only appear in their personal account.

How do I Join Club Eurostar?

Joining is free and easy, you just need to create an account. From there, you’ll be able to access:

- Your travel history
- Your status and accompanying perks
- Your online membership card
- A record of your points

It’s about time you were rewarded for train travel.

Please note, travellers who previously had a MyThalysWorld account should have had their details automatically transferred across.

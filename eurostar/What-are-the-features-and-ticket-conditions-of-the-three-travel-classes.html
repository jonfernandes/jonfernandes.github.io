From 23 April, tickets will be available for travel in our new travel classes Eurostar Standard, Eurostar Plus and Eurostar Premier*
*For travel on or after 4 November

Eurostar Standard:
Your fast and flexible way to travel from city to city in Europe. 

Eurostar Plus:
Choose a little extra, including more space to work or relax and an extra comfy seat for the journey. 

Eurostar Premier (For travel on or after 4 November):
Our signature Eurostar experience for work trips or pleasure, designed around you. Sit back, relax, unwind and arrive. 

Travelling to and from London

Eurostar Standard

  • Exchange with no fee up to 1 hour before departure. You'll need to pay any difference in fare.

  • Refundable up to 7 days pre-departure for €/£25    

  • 2 pieces of luggage + 1 small daypack/handbag    

  • Buy drinks, meals and snacks on board   

  • Free onboard wi-fi 

  • UK & EU plug sockets   

  • Earn Club Eurostar points 

  • Taxi reservation 


Eurostar Plus 

  • Exchange with no fee up to 1 hour pre-departure. You'll need to pay any difference in fare.

  • Refundable up to 7 days pre-departure for €/£25  

  • A delicious light meal and drinks served at your seat 

  • 2 pieces of luggage + 1 small daypack/handbag 

  • Extra spacious seats 

  • Free onboard wi-fi 

  • UK & EU plug sockets 

  • Free magazines on board 

  • Earn Club Eurostar points 

  • Taxi reservation 


Eurostar Premier (For travel on or after 4 November)

  • Exchangeable and refundable with no fee up to 2 days after departure 

  • Same-day boarding guarantee 

  • Exclusive lounge access in London, Paris and Brussels 

  • Priority ticket gates 

  • Extra spacious seats 

  • At-seat fine dining and drinks served by your dedicated onboard team 

  • 3 pieces of luggage + 1 small daypack/handbag 

  • Free onboard wi-fi 

  • UK + EU plug sockets 

  • Free newspapers and magazines 

  • Earn Club Eurostar points 

  • Taxi reservation

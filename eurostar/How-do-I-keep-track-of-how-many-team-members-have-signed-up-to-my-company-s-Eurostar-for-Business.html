How do I keep track of how many team members have signed up to my company’s Eurostar for Business?

When you login your admin profile you will have a list of ‘Joined’ and ‘Invited’ team members.  To navigate there click ‘Invited’ or ‘Joined’ on the navigation.

Invite requests sent are valid for 72 hours. If the team member did not activate the email link within this period, you can resend from the ‘Invited’ list.

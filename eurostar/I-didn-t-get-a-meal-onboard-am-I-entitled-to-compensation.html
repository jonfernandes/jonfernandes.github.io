I didn't get a meal in Standard Premier or Business Premier, am I entitled to compensation?

In the event that the meal in Standard Premier or Business Premier can’t be provided, compensation may apply.
 
Eurostar will offer:

  • e-voucher worth 15% of the affected leg for Standard Premier & Business Premier when no meal is provided

  • e-voucher worth 10% of affected leg for Standard Premier & Business Premier when a full service is not provided
     

You can Contact Us to claim your compensation.

Please note: catering that forms part of the bar buffet service is not subject to our compensation policy in the event of unavailability.

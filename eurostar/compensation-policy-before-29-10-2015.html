Additional expenses

In the case of late arrivals or where customers are going to be unable to complete their journey on the same day, we will pay certain expenses incurred as follows:

Hotel/accommodation – up to £150/€170 per room, per night

Taxi or local transport – Reasonable costs, but generally up to £50 per person. Payment of higher costs will be at Traveller Care’s discretion

Meals – We are not legally obliged to pay for these but again, reasonable costs will be considered. As a rule of thumb, up to £50 per person, per day is considered reasonable

In all cases above, receipts should be obtained for submission to Traveller Care.

Frequent Travellers (not covered by PRR)

FTP or Carte Blanche customers have the option to get the Eurostar compensation in points on their account if they wish to. Not applicable for the Eurostar Plus Points programme.

PRR refunds on through-tickets

Please note that the calculation of PRR refund amounts on through tickets is too complicated to explain here. A full explanation is given in section 7.4 of the compensation policy on Alfresco.  We can find out about the delays on other TGV trains either on this page of the SNCF's website, by using 'La Vie des Trains' or by e-mailing the CRE at ico@eurostar.com. For Belgian train delays (BE or NS IC trains, Thalys or ICE services departing/arriving at Belgian stations) we can use this website to check: http://www.railtime.be/website/home-en however it only works for up to 1 week in the past. You can also ask Pierre Raepsaet (preferrably in French), who's e-mail address is: rt.international@b-rail.be

More complicated issues

For any issue raised by a customer which is not mentioned in the table above, we do not have a strict policy; we have to judge each case on its merits. A good rule of thumb is that we compensate if the customer has been inconvenienced in some way and this was our fault.

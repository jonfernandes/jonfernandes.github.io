e320 - How will the wi-fi work? Can they browse the web and check emails?

We know how much our customers are looking forward to staying connected en route, so we’ve made sure we’ll have free wi-fi throughout the train. 

Behind the scenes, our internet connection will be delivered using 3G and 4G mobile networks. As such, connectivity will vary, but the good news is that most of the London-Paris route is covered. 

To make sure our internet connection doesn’t get overloaded and slow down, we’ll have a fair use policy in place. This means:

1. We’ll block websites like Netflix or YouTube that use up a lot of data and bandwidth. 
2. There’ll be download limits and once a customer reaches their data allowance, their connection speed will slow down. 
3. Business Premier and Standard Premier travellers will have greater download limits than those in Standard coaches. 

To help us continuously improve our wi-fi service, we’ll be proactively monitoring customer use and feedback and adjust our settings if we need to.

I can't purchase my subscription pass on eurostar.com, but can I use the website to book Frequent and Premium Pass tickets?

Yes, you can continue to buy tickets at the discounted rate and enjoy all the benefits that come with a Frequent or Premium Pass.

Book your ticket as normal by:
•    Logging into your account
•    Selecting the destination and date of travel
•    Selecting the Frequent or Premium Pass option
•    Purchasing your ticket at the discounted rate

If you can’t view your subscription pass in your account, please get in touch. We’ll be happy to do some digging and ensure your pass is visible again.

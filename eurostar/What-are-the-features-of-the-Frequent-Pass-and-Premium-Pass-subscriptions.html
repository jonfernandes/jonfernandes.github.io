What are the features of the Frequent Pass and Premium Pass subscriptions?

Travelling between Belgium, France, the Netherlands and Germany

If you travel a lot for work, or just love to explore Europe, the Frequent and Premium Passes are a game-changer.

Designed for those who travel regularly in Europe, these subscription passes offer a range of perks and discounts. 

What are the benefits of a Frequent Pass?

- Available across all of Europe
- 50% discount off the highest ticket price, up to seven days before the date of departure
- 30% discount on tickets purchased after the seven day deadline and up until the time of departure
- Automatic access to Club Eurostar’s Avantage tier and all its accompanying benefits
- Tickets are exchangeable without a fee until one hour after departure and 100% refundable until one day after departure*
- Access to framing trains (trains immediately before or after the train you had originally booked).

*See terms and conditions

What are the benefits of a Premium Pass?

This subscription is the perfect pass if you travel regularly between Paris (Roissy CDG, Marnes-La-Vallée, Paris Nord) and Belgium (Brussels, Liège, Antwerp).

- €33 per single journey
- Access to all trains at any time of day (conditions apply)
- Tickets are exchangeable without a fee until one hour after departure and 100% refundable until one day after departure
- Access to Club Eurostar’s Carte Blanche tier and all its accompanying benefits

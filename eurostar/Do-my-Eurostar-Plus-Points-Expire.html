Do my Eurostar Plus Points Expire?

With Eurostar™ Plus Points you earn one point for every £1/€1 you spend on eurostar.com. Every time you reach 300 points, we’ll send you a £20/€20 voucher to use on any Eurostar ticket within 12 months. Easy.
 
If you don’t reach 300 points within an 18 month period, your points will expire. So it’s worth keeping an eye on your total to make sure you don’t miss out.

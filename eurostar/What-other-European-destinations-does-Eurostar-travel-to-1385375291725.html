What other European destinations does Eurostar travel to?

Eurostar travels directly between the UK, France and Belgium and we can also book onward tickets to over 100 European destinations from most mainline UK stations. Eurostar direct services run from London St Pancras International, Ebbsfleet International and Ashford International to Calais-Fréthun, Lille Europe, Paris Gare du Nord, Brussels Midi and Disneyland® Resort Paris (Marne la Vallée). We also have a direct service to the French Alps and a connecting service to the Swiss Alps in the winter months and a summer service to Avignon. To find out where we can take you, just head over to Eurostar.com or check out our Connecting Information page.

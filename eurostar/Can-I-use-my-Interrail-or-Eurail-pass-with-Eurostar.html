Can I use my Interrail or Eurail pass with Eurostar?

Yes. We’re all friends, so of course you can use your Interrail or Eurail pass on Eurostar trains.

Travelling between Belgium, France, the Netherlands and Germany

To travel with your Interrail or Eurail pass, you must book a ticket using the Interrail or Eurail fare.
This ticket, which can only be purchased by telephone and at specific stations (NS/SNCB or SNCF), automatically includes a seat reservation.
Note these passes are not valid on Eurostar Sun and Snow trains.

Travelling to and from London

To travel on Eurostar trains with your Interrail or Eurail pass, you must reserve a seat, and pay a booking fee (price differs depending on travel class).
We’d recommend booking your tickets to and from London in advance.

Eurail and Interrail passes are valid for travel between the following countries:
• France and the United Kingdom
• Belgium and the United Kingdom
• The Netherlands and the United Kingdom

Don't forget to bring your pass with you when you travel and have it ready to show at the ticket barriers.
If it hasn't been validated yet, just arrive a little earlier on the day and head to the ticket office where we'll stamp it for you.

I can’t seem to watch videos on BBC, CNN etc. Why is that?


To make sure everyone can enjoy a seamless connection, we’ll be limiting access to some news sites that have a lot video content (like cnn.com and bbc.co.uk). You’ll find Euronews and magazines on our free onboard entertainment system, which has no usage restrictions.

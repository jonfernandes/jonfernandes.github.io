Can I claim points when someone else books a ticket for me?

Yes, we like to share.

For a train only booking if your Club Eurostar number or Customer Identification Number (CIN) is entered by the booker, you’ll get the points. All you have to do is complete the journey.

If you’ve booked a Train + Hotel package, only the lead traveller on the list will receive the Reward and Status points. Only one membership number can be linked to each booking.

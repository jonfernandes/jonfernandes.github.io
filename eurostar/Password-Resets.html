Password Reset Requests

The customer should always be advised to self-serve online (password reset functionality on eurostar.com). Go through the process step-by-step and ensure the process is followed correctly. 
If this is not an option, and the advisor has ensured they have followed the correct steps, the advisor may do this on their behalf.

If the above has not yielded any results, please follow the process below:

0. Advise the customer to re-register first, to ensure they exist in Cognito.

1. Advisor to check Starlight in “MC Mails Sent” for the password reset email

⮚If the Status = Sent AND the Status Reason = Sent, then the mail has been sent

⮚Advisor to advise customer to check junk/deleted folders firstly and secondly to check whether their ISP is blocking the mail from noreply@eurostar.com

⮚If the customer has exhausted all options, then advise to use a different email address

2. If:

⮚Status of the email says anything other than Sent

⮚The email does not show under “MC Mails Sent”

⮚Another error is displayed along the path

…. then raise a Business Improvement Intake Fom.

NOTE:
The reset password functionality on Eurostar.com is no different from what Application Support can do behind the scenes. Therefore asking App Support to do a password reset has no impact if customer/advisor have already done so on the website. A password reset failing is a symptom of another underlying issue that would need investigating.

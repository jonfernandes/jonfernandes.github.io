Can I access the Eurostar lounges?

Access to our lounges depends on your route, ticket and member status. Upon entry, you can enjoy serene spaces, ideal for working or relaxing.

Travelling between Belgium, France, the Netherlands and Germany

Bruxelles-Midi and Paris-Nord lounges are accessible to:

Avantage members
Carte Blanche members with 2 guests
Etoile members with 4 guests

Partner lounges
Travellers with a same-day Premium ticket have access to:

Paris-Nord and Brussels-Midi lounges

NS International – Regus Express lounges at Amsterdam, Rotterdam and Schiphol

Club Eurostar
Avantage, Carte Blanche and Etoile members have access to:

Paris-Nord and Brussels-Midi lounges

Carte Blanche and Etoile members have access to:

DB lounges at Cologne, Düsseldorf and Essen

NS International – Regus Express lounges at Amsterdam, Rotterdam and Schiphol

Railteam lounges in France (SNCF), Belgium, Switzerland (SBB) and Austria (ÖBB).
 

Travelling to and from London

Carte Blanche and Etoile members and those travelling in Business Premier have access to all our lounges.

Carte Blanche members may bring one guest during the week (Monday to Friday) and up to four guests at weekends (Saturdays and Sundays) into Business Premier Lounges.

Étoile members may bring two guests during the week (Monday to Friday) and up to four guests at weekends (Saturdays and Sundays) into Business Premier Lounges.

e320 - Step by step process for accessing the on-board entertainment

Download the Eurostar app before travelling
Once on board, connect to the Eurostar wi-fi as follows: 

1.    Go to your network settings.
2.    Find your wi-fi settings and choose EurostarTrainsWiFi.
3.    A form will pop up. Just fill it in to register and click Submit. You’ll only need to register once. 
4.    Click Connect on the next page and you’re online.
5.    If you’re on a mobile, open the Eurostar app for all our TV programmes, movies, games and more. If you’re on a laptop, just go to onboard.eurostar.com to access the content. 

If the registration or Connect page doesn’t pop up automatically, just go to onboard.eurostar.com in your web browser as soon as you are connected to the wi-fi.

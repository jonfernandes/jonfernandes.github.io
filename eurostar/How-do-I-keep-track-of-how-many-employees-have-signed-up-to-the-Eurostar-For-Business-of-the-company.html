How do I keep track of how many employees have signed up to the Eurostar For Business of the company?

Under ‘Company Account’ on your Eurostar For Business home page, select ‘Employees’ where you will have a list of ‘Joined’ and ‘Invited’ employees.
Requests sent are valid for 24 hours. If the employee did not activate the email link within this period, you can resend from the ‘Invited’ list.

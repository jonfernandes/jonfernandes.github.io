Can I take a kid’s bike trailer on Eurostar?

Yes, you can send a bike trailer from London, Paris, Brussels or Lille with EuroDespatch, our registered luggage service, subject to availability.
 
Just leave a little extra time to drop it off before you board and we’ll get it on the next available train, guaranteeing it arrives in your destination within 24 hours.
 
Otherwise, you can drop it off up to 48 hours before you travel and we’ll keep it at our luggage office in your destination for up to two weeks.

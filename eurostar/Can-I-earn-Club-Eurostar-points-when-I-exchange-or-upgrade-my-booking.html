Can I earn Club Eurostar points when I exchange or upgrade my booking?

Yes. If you make any changes or upgrades online, the points will be added to your account. Customers cannot earn points when taking advantage of our impulse upgrades at the station, onboard our trains or if you upgrade after your ticket has been scanned at the ticket gates.

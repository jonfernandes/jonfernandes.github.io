How do I book my taxi as a Eurostar carte blanche holder?

As a Eurostar carte blanche holder, you may pre-book a taxi for your arrival. Where it's available Pre-bookable taxis are available for arrivals at St Pancras International, Ebbsfleet International, Ashford International, Paris Gare du Nord and Brussels-Midi/Zuid. How to book Show your card in the Business Premier lounge (or at the information desk at Ashford International) and our staff will book you a taxi and give you a voucher. At arrivals (or at the drop-off area outside Ebbsfleet International) hand this voucher to the driver holding a card with your name. How to pay Pay the driver directly when you arrive at your final destination. Prices start from £25 in London and €50 in Brussels. In Paris payment is by the meter, with an extra charge of €10-15 to cover the call out and waiting time.

Can I use my Eurostar e-voucher to pay for Train + Hotel bookings?

Yes, you can use your e-vouchers as part payment towards Train + Hotel bookings. To use your voucher: 

1.       Select Use voucher in the payment section on the checkout page.
2.       Enter the 9-digit e-voucher reference in the Voucher number box.
3.       Hit Apply.
4.       Check the voucher’s been deducted from the total at the bottom of the page.

If you have problems using your voucher, check it’s still valid and is for a value less than your booking total. If you’re still having problems, contact us on 03432 186 186.

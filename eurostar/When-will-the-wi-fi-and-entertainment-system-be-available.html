When will the wi-fi and entertainment system be available

When will the wi-fi and entertainment system be available?
Our wi-fi and entertainment system is now up and running on all of our new trains, so you can connect to a great range of entertainment, including TV programmes, movies, news and more.

What content’s available?
There’s something for everyone, including films, TV programmes, magazines, games and documentaries. There’s also plenty to keep the kids amused. 

Which languages is the onboard entertainment available in?
Our free onboard entertainment is currently available in French and English on new and refurbished trains travelling between London and Paris. 

How often will the content change?
We’ll keep track of what travellers think and update the content regularly throughout the year.

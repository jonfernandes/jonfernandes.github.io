Can I share my Club Eurostar points and what are the limits when sharing?

You can share your points with anyone who’s in Club Eurostar. Points shared do not count towards the recipient’s club level.
 
We do have some limits with sharing and receiving points. Here are the details:

  • Points have to be shared in multiples of 100.
  • You can share a maximum of 3,600 points in one go.
  • You can share a maximum of 7,500 points in total per calendar year.
  • You can share points with the same member a maximum of four times per calendar year.
  • You can receive a maximum of 7,500 points per calendar year.

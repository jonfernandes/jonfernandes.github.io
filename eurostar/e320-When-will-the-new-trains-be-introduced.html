e320 - When will the new trains be introduced?

The first e300 started running in August and the next is due in May 2016, with more e300s being worked on and rolled out by March 2018. 

We’ll start introducing our new e320s from 20 November 2015 on the London-Paris route. We’ll initially have one commercial e320 running, then ramp up to four for the Christmas and New Year period. 

From Jan 2016, 50% of Paris trains will be e320s. This means that our travellers will have a 50% chance of taking an e320 between Paris and London, and 80% by Easter 2016. 

By the end of 2016, the Paris-London route will be 100% e320. 

In 2017 we’ll start running e320s on our Brussels route, with 30% by Q3 and 80% by Q4 that year. The Amsterdam route is also planned to start in 2017. 

By March 2018 all Eurostar journeys will be on e300s and e320s.

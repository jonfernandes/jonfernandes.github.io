How do I book a taxi as a Eurostar carte blanche member?

As a Eurostar carte blanche member, you may pre-book a taxi for your arrival.

Where is it available?
Pre-bookable taxis are available for arrivals at

  • St Pancras International
  • Ebbsfleet International
  • Ashford International
  • Paris Gare du Nord
  • Brussels-Midi/Zuid

How do I book?
Show your card in the Business Premier lounge (or at the information desk at Ashford International) and our staff will book you a taxi and give you a voucher. At arrivals (or at the drop-off area outside Ebbsfleet International) hand this voucher to the driver holding a card with your name.

How do I pay?
Pay the driver directly when you arrive at your final destination. Prices start from £25* in London and €50* in Brussels. In Paris payment is by the meter, with an extra charge of €10-15* to cover the call out and waiting time.

* Prices subject to change.

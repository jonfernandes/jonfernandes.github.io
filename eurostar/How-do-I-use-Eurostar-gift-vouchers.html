How do I use Eurostar gift vouchers?

Paper gift vouchers can only be used on train journeys, not on bookings that include a hotel or car hire.

Paper gift vouchers
To make a booking using a gift voucher, please email us or come into the ticket office at St Pancras International. 

Although we no longer sell paper gift vouchers, we’ll honour those that are still valid. Please send us your vouchers well before you travel. We will then be able to release your booking.

Eurostar e-vouchers
If you have an e-voucher following a delay, you can use it to make a booking at eurostar.com.

How do I find Eurostar departures at the station?

At St Pancras International:
Once inside St Pancras International, follow the signs to ‘International Departures’.

At Paris Gare du Nord:

At Brussels-Midi/Zuid:
Once inside the station, follow signs to ‘Channel Terminal’.
If you’re travelling to Rotterdam and Amsterdam (not to the UK), just make your way straight to the platform. Please arrive at the station 20 minutes before departure and board the train straight away. Our team will check your ticket at the doors in Standard Premier and on board in Standard. .

At Amsterdam Centraal:
If you’re travelling to London, make your way to the Eurostar departure building on platform 15b.
If you’re travelling to Brussels, please go straight to platform 15 – there’s no need to go to the Eurostar departure building.

At Rotterdam Centraal:
If you’re travelling to London, make your way to the Eurostar departure building on platform 2.
If you’re travelling to Brussels, please go straight to platform 2 – there’s no need to go to the Eurostar departure building.

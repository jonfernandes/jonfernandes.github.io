How do I earn and spend Eurostar Plus Points e-vouchers?

You’ll earn a £20 e-voucher for every £300 you spend on eurostar.com. Just remember to tap in your membership number every time you book, then we’ll send you a voucher to spend on a future booking.

Just check under the e-voucher tab when logged into your account to see how many vouchers you have earned.

Account Mergers

  1. Advisor to ensure that Mr Smith has transferred the points to one account (if relevant to case)
  2. Advisor to instruct Mr Smith to close the unwanted account (if relevant to case)
  3. Advisor to write clear notes on Starlight case and then assign to the Starlight case to the Online Accounts Helpdesk (OAH) queue to perform the merge in Starlight
  4. Once confirmation has been received from the OAH that the merger has been completed, Advisor to raise a MyITCognito Email Change form
  5. Application Support updates the identity and profile for Mr Smith in Cognito and confirms to Advisor when complete
  6. Advisor to inform Mr Smith now has access to his account under email B. Mr Smith can change his password using the self-service functionality if he wishes to do so.

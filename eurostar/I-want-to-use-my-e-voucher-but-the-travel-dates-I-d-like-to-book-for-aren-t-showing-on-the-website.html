I want to use my e-voucher but the travel dates I’d like to book for aren’t showing on the website

Please note that our timetables are released throughout the year. Some trains go on sale as early as 11 months in advance, with additional trains added about 6 months before the departure date. This is the case for our trains between London and Lille, Paris, Brussels, Rotterdam or Amsterdam.

If you’re booking a connecting journey with a change in Lille, Paris or Brussels, tickets will go on sale later, usually about 4 months in advance.
Just make sure you book your train online before the date shown on your e-voucher.

Can I get an invoice for my ticket?

Your ticket is your invoice.

Just hop onto your account via eurostar.com, log in to manage your booking, scroll to **payment history** and click **print receipt.**

All accounting details can be found on your ticket. Your tickets will be accepted as accounting evidence with all European tax authorities.

If you booked with an external agency or third-party business, please contact them directly for an invoice or receipt.

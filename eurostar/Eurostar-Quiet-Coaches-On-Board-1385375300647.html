Eurostar Quiet Coaches On-Board

GENERAL From 11 September 2012 Eurostar will be introducing quiet coaches on all its trains. This will be a free service available to Business Premier and Standard Premier customers when they select a seat in coach 8 or 11. These will be permanently branded as quiet coaches on all trains, although there will be exceptions on some services. What are quiet coaches? The quiet coaches will provide a peaceful environment where people can use their time on board as they wish, be it to work or relax.

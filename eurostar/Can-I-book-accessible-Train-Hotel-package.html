Can I book accessible Train + Hotel package?

We are unable to take bookings online for Train + Hotel packages with wheelchair spaces as we need to ensure we partner your train booking with one of our carefully chosen accessible hotels. Please take a look online and use the relevant filters to check for hotels that have Disabled Access and Disabled Facilities to give you an idea of hotels available for the dates that you want to travel.

e320 - Will mobility scooters be taken on board the e320?

Yes - Travellers need to book their mobility scooter through Eurodespatch. 

Smaller mobility scooters (up to 70cm wide and 100cm long) will fit in our wheelchair spaces on the e320. 

If the mobility scooter is bigger than 70 x 100 cm, Eurodespatch will advise if the larger mobility scooters can be accepted in the hold of an e300 or TMST.

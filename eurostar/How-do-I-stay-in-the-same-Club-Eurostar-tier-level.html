How do I stay in the same Club Eurostar tier level?

To stay in Classique, you don’t need to do anything as there’s no minimum number of Status points required.

To stay in Avantage, earn between 500 and 2899 Status points from Eurostar travel within your membership year.

To stay in Carte Blanche, earn between 2,900 to 4,999 Status points from Eurostar travel within your membership year.

To stay in Étoile, earn over 5000 points from Eurostar travel within your membership year.

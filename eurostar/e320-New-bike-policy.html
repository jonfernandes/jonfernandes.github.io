e320 - Bike policy

We have reverted to our former policy which is as follows:
  • Folding bicycles can be taken on any Eurostar train as long as they are smaller than 85cm when fully folded
  • Before going through check-in they also need to be in a protective case that covers the whole bike
  • All non-folding bikes or those over 85cm long need to travel with our registered luggage
  • To book your bike before you travel email our Eurodespatch team eurodespatch@eurostar.com or call 03448 225 822
  • To book on the day go straight to Eurodespatch when you arrive at the station
  • Spaces for fully mounted bikes will be allocated on a first come first served basis as we have a limited number of spaces
  • Alternatively you can put your bike in a padded bike bag or bike box
  • We can provide a box for you which is included in the service price and this is the best option for protecting your bike

I’ve booked from one station, can I board at another?

We're unable to run our services from and to Ashford International and Ebbsfleet International stations this year. As it is not be possible to take the Eurostar from or to these stations, you'll need to travel from or to London St Pancras International.

Tickets booked from the Continent to the UK
You may only board the train at the station shown on your ticket.

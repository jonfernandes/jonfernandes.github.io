Is there a minimum age for joining Club Eurostar?

Yes, from 7 February 2023 travellers must be at least 16 years of age to join. From then, you can enjoy all the benefits of Club Eurostar.

Children aged 4-15 who joined the Club Eurostar programme before 7 February 2023, will still keep their membership.

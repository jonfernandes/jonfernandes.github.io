How do I access onboard entertainment?

Once you’re on the train, you’ll be able to connect to wi-fi and a great range of entertainment, including TV programmes, movies, news and more.

  1. Go to your wi-fi settings and choose EurostarTrainsWiFi.

  2.  A form will pop up. Just fill it in and click Submit. 

  3. Click Connect on the next page to get online.

  4. If you’re on a mobile or Android tablet, open the Eurostar app to enjoy all of our TV programmes, films, games and more. If you’re on a laptop, iPad or don't have the app, just go to onboard.eurostar.com. 


If the registration page doesn’t pop up automatically, just go to register.onboard.eurostar.com in your web browser.

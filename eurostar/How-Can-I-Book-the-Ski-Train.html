How Can I Book the Ski Train?

We’ve teamed up with Travelski, who’ll be offering direct trains to the Alps as part of a package.

For more information, visit Travelski’s website or call them on 0800 260 5082 (Mon to Fri from 09:00 – 19.30, or Sat from 09.30 – 19:00).

Just want to book train tickets?

You can also hit the slopes by taking the Eurostar to Paris, then hopping on a TGV train to Moûtiers and Bourg-Saint-Maurice.

Travelling to Disneyland® Paris?

When can I book the direct service to Disney? The Disney train runs daily between London and Disneyland® Paris, leaving St Pancras at around 10:17. The journey takes 2h35, calling at Ashford International and Lille Europe. On the inbound the Disney train leaves Marne-la-Vallée at around 18:02. The Disney service can be booked further in advance of our standard 120 day booking horizon. Please note: The direct Disney train does not run on Tuesdays and Saturdays, except on the following dates. Yes, you have two options: a) You can travel with the TGV from Lille with a quick and easy exchange in Lille Europe, just changing platforms! However, you can only book these tickets on Eurostar.com 90 days in advance. TGV services to Marne-la-Vallée are very frequent and normally take around 1h10; however some services can take longer. b) If you need to book more in advance, you can book your normal ticket with Eurostar to Paris 120 days in advance. Disneyland® resort is easy to reach from central Paris by taking the RER. More details: Line: RER A (Red) Direction: Marne-la-Vallée (RER line A4) Departure Stations : Charles de Gaulle Etoile, Auber, Chatelet Les Halles, Gare de Lyon, Nation. The best option is to take the RER D or B from Gare du Nord to Chatelet Les Halles and change for the RER A to Marne-la-Vallée. Price: €6.45 (as of July 1, 2008) (Zone 1 -> Zone 5) Travel Time: 39-45 minutes Frequency of trains: approx. every 15 min. (9 minutes to 30 minutes between trains) You can also purchase a day pass to the resort including the RER transfer from the information desk in the departure lounges at St Pancras, Ashford International and Ebbsfleet International.

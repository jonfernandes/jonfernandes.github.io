After investigating the logs and the Tealeaf session, we found out that during the booking process, the session had expired on the payment page.The user was present on the payment page for around 10 mins.The user was then redirected to the home page as the session was expired and so the cancel PNR request was executed.Then the user must have pressed the back button or by some way or the other gone back to the payment page.Then on submission of that page, as the PNR has already been cancelled, the user gets SBE-301 which is SBE_BOOKEDPNR_NOT_FOUND_OR_DATA_INVALID. or EIF_132

Are you restricting data usage or access to some websites? Can I access Netflix on board?


To make sure everyone can enjoy a seamless connection, we limit connection speed and access to some video and music streaming sites, as well as those with content that’s not suitable for an open public network. We also limit access to some news sites that have a lot video content like cnn.com and bbc.co.uk.

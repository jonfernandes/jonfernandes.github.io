How far in advance can I make a booking on Eurostar?

Most Eurostar services can be booked up to 120 days in advance of the return date of travel. Journeys that involve a connection with the French national railway network can be booked up to 90 days in advance of the return date of travel. To calculate when you will be able to book simply count the appropriate number of days back from your return date of travel. Direct services to Disneyland Resort Paris have a longer booking period and are made available further in advance by calling 08432 186 186. Our direct seasonal services to the French Alps and Avignon are opened for sale for the entire season. These normally go on sale a few months before the service starts. Please refer to our web site for more information. If there is no availability shown for the fare or trains required on the day selected, it may be that you are booking too far ahead. Please note: We are unable to offer quotes or train timings on future journeys that fall outside of the booking horizon.

How far in advance can I book Eurostar tickets?

From 23 April, tickets on our trains travelling to or from London or between France, Belgium, the Netherlands and Germany can be booked between 6 and 8 months in advance.

Sometimes these times can vary, for instance if there’s engineering work on one of the lines.

Eurostar Snow Trains
Tickets for our Snow Train usually go on sale in August, but please keep an eye on our dedicated page for updates.

Disneyland® Paris
We are currently not running a direct train from London to Disneyland® Paris. However, Eurostar tickets from London to Disneyland® Paris via Lille usually go on sale 4 months in advance.

Connections with other train companies

TGV
Journeys with a connection to a TGV train in France can usually be booked up to 120 days in advance but this can vary.

Deutsche Bahn ICE
Journeys connecting to Deutsche Bahn InterCity Express trains to Belgium and Germany can usually be booked up to 90 days before travel but this can vary.

UK National Rail
Connecting journeys in the UK usually go on sale around 84 days in advance but this varies between train operating companies.

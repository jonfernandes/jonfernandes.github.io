When will wi-fi be available?

Our wi-fi and entertainment system is now switched on all of our new trains.

How can I connect ?
Here’s how:

  1. Go to your wi-fi settings and choose EurostarTrainsWiFi.

  2. A form will pop up. Just fill it in  and click Submit.

  3. Click Connect on the next page to get online.

  4. If you’re on a mobile or Android tablet, open the Eurostar app to enjoy all our TV programmes, films, games and more. If you’re on a laptop, iPad or if you do not have the app, just go to onboard.eurostar.com.  



If the registration or Connect page doesn’t pop up automatically, just go to register.onboard.eurostar.com in your web browser.

Is it available on all routes?
Free onboard wi-fi is currently available on our new and refurbished trains travelling between London and Paris. As more of our new and refurbished trains roll out, our wi-fi connection will start to cover more of our routes.

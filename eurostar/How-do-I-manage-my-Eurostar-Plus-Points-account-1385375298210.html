How do I manage my Eurostar Plus Points account?

I'm having problems with my Eurostar Plus Points account. Who do I need to contact? If you are experiencing problems with your Eurostar Plus Points account then please contact us using the 'Email Us' tab on the left column. There's a spelling mistake in my name - What do I need to do to correct this? As a member you can update your personal details online by logging into your account and clicking on Profile and Preferences' How do I receive a written statement of all of my account transactions? Since Eurostar Plus Points is a loyalty scheme which operates entirely online, as a member you will need to log into your Eurostar Plus Points account online to view all transactions as no statements are sent via post.

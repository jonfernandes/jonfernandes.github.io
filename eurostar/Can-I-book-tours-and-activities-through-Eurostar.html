Can I book tours and activities through Eurostar?

Yes. We’ve teamed up with Ceetiz to help you find and book activities and experiences in some of Europe’s top cities.

From tours and shows to priority access to top attractions, everything’s covered by a best price guarantee, and it’s quick and easy to book online before or during your trip.

To book, just go to eurostar.ceetiz.com to see what’s on, where. Note all activities and experiences are booked through, and provided by Ceetiz.com.

What is a companion voucher and how do they work?

If you’re an Étoile member, you’ll be allocated two companion vouchers each membership year.

Each voucher allows you to book a complementary return journey for a travel companion when booking a reward ticket for yourself in Standard, Standard Premier or Comfort. You needn’t book both tickets at the same time – you can add a companion ticket to an existing booking later. But you must travel on the same train.

No digital or physical vouchers are issued, and you can make a companion booking by getting in touch with our Contact Centre.

Any vouchers not used by the end of your membership year will not be carried over. And any reward tickets and companion tickets booked together will be non-exchangeable and non-refundable.

Part of my journey is with another train company. Will I earn Club Eurostar points for the whole journey?

If you purchase a combined Eurostar and TGV ticket, you’ll earn reward points and status points based on the total ticket price.

However, if you purchase an ‘Any Belgian Station’ ticket or a combined Eurostar and Deutsche Bahn ticket, you’ll earn reward points and status points based solely on the Eurostar leg of your journey.

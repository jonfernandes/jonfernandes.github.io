e320 - What is our luggage policy for the e320?

While designing our new trains, we made sure we could keep our competitive advantage over airlines, with their strict size and weight restrictions, without compromising other features.

For the majority, the basic luggage allowance of two 85cm items plus a small piece of hand luggage per person has not changed. However, if you are travelling with a Business Premier ticket, you will now be entitled to take 3 pieces of luggage with you.

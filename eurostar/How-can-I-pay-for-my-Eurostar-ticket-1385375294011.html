How can I pay for my Eurostar ticket?

Tickets purchased via Eurostar.Com must be paid for by credit or debit card or eVoucher at the time of booking. We accept the following Credit and debit cards: Mastercard, Visa, American Express, Maestro (GBP only), Switch (GBP only), Visa Electron(GBP only) and Delta (GBP only) cards. If you choose to collect your tickets on departure from the Eurostar terminal you will be required to present the credit or debit card used for payment, however, your card will be debited with the full amount at the time of booking. If you do not have a credit or debit card, you can still purchase tickets with cash or cheque at the Eurostar ticket desks at St Pancras International, Ebbsfleet International or Ashford International and Paris and Brussels stations. Some travel agents and local railway stations are also able to sell Eurostar tickets.

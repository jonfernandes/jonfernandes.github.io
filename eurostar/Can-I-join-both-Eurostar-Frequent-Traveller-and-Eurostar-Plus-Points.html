Can I join both Eurostar Frequent Traveller and Eurostar Plus Points?

No, you’ll need to pick the programme that’s best for you. 

As a quick guide, to join Eurostar™ Frequent Traveller, you need to spend at least £250 on a journey (one-way or return). 

You can join Eurostar Plus Points and start collecting points when you book any ticket.

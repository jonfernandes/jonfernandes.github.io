Will I earn Club Eurostar points if I book a standalone hotel on Eurostar.com?

No, but you can earn points on a Trains + Hotel booking.

How many Club Eurostar points will I earn on a Trains + Hotel booking?

For every €2 spent you will earn 1 point. And for every £2 spent you will earn 1.2 points.
Reward points and Status points will only be awarded to the lead passenger on a Train + Hotel booking.

Only one membership number can be linked to each booking and the other travellers on the booking will not be entitled to points.
The higher your tier, the more reward points you receive on eligible bookings.

Classique: none
Avantage: +25%
Carte blanche: +50%
Étoile: +75%

The bonus point multipliers above do not apply to your Status points balance.

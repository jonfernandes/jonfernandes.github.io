How does Eurostar Odyssey work?

Exclusively available from your seat on board, you can enjoy your virtual reality experience with a smartphone or tablet. Here’s how:

  1. Once you’re on the train, connect to EurostarTrainsWiFi.

  2. Open the Eurostar app or go to onboard.eurostar.com.

  3. Tap Eurostar Odyssey to start your adventure.

  4. Choose device-only view and dive into the 360° action by moving your phone from left to right, up and down.

 
If you’re using a virtual reality cardboard headset, choose headset view then pop your phone into the headset for a fully immersive experience.
 
You’ll need a pair of headphones but there’s no need to stand up. You can enjoy the full Eurostar underwater adventure from your seat, simply looking up, down and all around.

Why do child National rail tickets and Eurostar tickets have different age restrictions?

National Rail child tickets are available for kids from 5 to 16 years old and children under 5 travel free. Anyone 16 years old or over is classified as an adult. On Eurostar services, Child tickets are for 4 to 11 years olds and we also have Youth tickets for 12 to 25 year olds, so you can enjoy discounted tickets for longer. Little ones under 4* travel for free on Eurostar services and won't need a ticket. Age groups are taken into account automatically by the booking system and your tickets will always be valid for the ages you tapped into the website. If you're travelling with children under 4 years old, you won't need to add them to your booking. * Free travel for children up to the age of 4 is limited to 1 child per adult. Any additional children may need to buy a ticket.

e320 - Are the new trains specifically for new routes?

Initially the e320s will start operating on the London to Paris route, and will start serving Brussels and Amsterdam from 2017.   
As we develop new routes and destinations we will assess the best type of set to suit that service, both from a commercial, technical and operational perspective.
 Our e320s are interoperable which means they have the ability to operate on parts of the European network which our current trains cannot serve, e.g. Amsterdam

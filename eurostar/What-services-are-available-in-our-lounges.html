What services are available in our lounges?

An oasis away from the hustle and bustle, our lounges offer exclusivity and comfort, these spaces are ideal for work or pleasure.
There’s a wide selection of digital newspapers and magazines, wi-fi and electrical outlets are of course provided too.
Travel information is displayed on screens and our staff are on hand for any queries or requests.
All our lounges are located within the stations and close to Eurostar platforms.

Travelling between Belgium, France, the Netherlands and Germany

Food and drink
Hot drinks, snacks and nibbles are provided in our European lounges.

Meetings
A fully-equipped meeting room seating six people is available free of charge. This includes everything you might need: projector screen, whiteboard and of course, fast wi-fi.

Luggage
We only accept folding bikes or bicycles transported in a non-rigid case, in our lounges. Non-folded bicycles or bicycles not transported in a case must remain outside.
All luggage is the owner’s responsibility at all times.

Pets
Small dogs and other pets are allowed in our lounges on European routes, but cannot roam freely. Common sense rules apply.
Pets must weigh less than 6 kg and travel in a container with maximum dimensions 45 x 30 x 25 cm.

Travelling to and from London

Food and drink
A continental breakfast, mid-morning snacks and sweet and savoury light bites are available - depending on the time of day.
An array of hot and cold drinks are served, while expert mixologists whip up creative cocktails.
Too early for a tipple? We also have a very popular smoothie bar, blending fresh fruit and veg between 06:00 and noon, Monday to Friday.

Pets
No pets are allowed in lounges on our London routes.

The art of travel
Our lounges showcase snippets of Europe's contemporary art scene. For years we’ve been working in partnership with Friday Trampoline – a collective of curators that specialises in unearthing early and mid-career artists.

Going green
It’s not just onboard that we are passionate about the planet. In our lounges we’ve replaced plastic bottles with water fountains saving 800,000 bottles of plastic a year. And all our onward menus are now available digitally, saving around 600,000 sheets of paper a year.

We have proudly received the highest 3-star rating from the Sustainable Restaurant Association.

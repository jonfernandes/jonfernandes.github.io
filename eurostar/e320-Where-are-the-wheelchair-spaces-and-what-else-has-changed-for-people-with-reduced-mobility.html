e320 - Where are the wheelchair spaces and what else has changed for people with reduced mobility?

In all of our new trains, wheelchair spaces are in Standard Premier and Business Premier. On the e320, there are two spaces in coach 3 and an additional two in coach 14. There are also four spaces in the e300 in coaches 9 and 10. 

For operational reasons, we’ll only sell two wheelchair spaces on each train for as long as we’re using TMSTs - one in each coach .

We’re also happy to say that 10% of the seats - known as "priority seats" - on our e320 and e300 have been designated as suitable for travellers with reduced mobility, and these include extra legroom for easier access. During the booking process, if a traveller identifies themselves as having reduced mobility, they can be offered one of these seats. Teams on board will also be able to reallocate these seats.

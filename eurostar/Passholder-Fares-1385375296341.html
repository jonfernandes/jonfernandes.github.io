Passholder Fares

Passholder fares cannot be booked online. They can only be booked at a Eurostar Terminal or through our contact centre on 08432 186 186 or +44 (0) 1233 617 575. All tickets are sold subject to availability. We offer a 'Passholder' fare for those passengers who are in possession of one of the following passes: Eurail Eurodomino (freedom) Benelux Tourail Britrail (BritFrance) Europass Inter-rail 26/+26 passes only The 'Passholder' fare is non-refundable but is exchangeable once before the departure of either leg, subject to seat availability at the time of request. The price of a 'Passholder' ticket travelling from the UK to the Continent start from £82 (Pounds) for a single or from £144 (Pounds) for a return ticket. The price of a 'Passholder' ticket travelling from the Continent to the UK start from £72 (Pounds) for a single or from £128 (Pounds) for a return ticket. As long as you are in possession of your pass on the day of travel, it is not necessary to validate it first.

Compensation new policy

After reviewing our compensation policy, we made a decision to make some changes.

Comparing our policy with other transport operators, we have been able to retain a generous policy as well as allowing the compensation to be used on any route bookable directly through us.

With this change, we are also now able to verify if customers are eligible for compensation via our websitewww.eurostar.com and issue any applicable e-vouchers straight away which are valid for one year.

Below you will find a copy of the new policy:

To calculate EFT compensation in points, you divide the delay eVoucher amount that the customer is due by 0.15. eg 60+ delay. Delay leg value is £36, eVoucher is £18 % 0.15 = 120 eft points. NB: If the ticket was booked in euros we would divide the eVoucher amount by 0.20 rather than 0.15 to calculate points due.

How long will my Eurostar frequent traveller membership last?

To maintain an active level of Eurostar frequent traveller membership:
You must register to your account, and travel on, a minimum of one single paid journey in your name during each membership year.

To maintain Eurostar carte blanche membership:
You must earn 1500 status points in each membership year. Status points can only be earned by Eurostar travel.

If you don't earn the required 1500 status points by the end of your membership year, then your status will revert to Eurostar carte classique.

Is Eurostar Odyssey safe for children?

Absolutely – little ones as young as two have tried and loved it. We recommend our youngest explorers ask for help from a grown-up to set up the experience though.

We just ask that adventurers of all ages enjoy their Eurostar Odyssey from their seat, rather than standing up or moving around the coach.

We’ve been careful to create an experience that shouldn’t cause motion sickness when using a virtual reality headset, including settings that let you choose the direction of travel that matches the way you’re actually facing on the train.

If using a virtual reality headset makes you feel queasy though, just try again without it.

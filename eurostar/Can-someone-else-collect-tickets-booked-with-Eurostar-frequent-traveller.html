Can someone else collect tickets booked with Eurostar frequent traveller?

Tickets must be collected by the credit card holder, at which time he or she will be asked to produce the relevant credit card and identification.

Please note that when making a booking less than 48 hours in advance of travel, you will only be given the option of collecting your tickets from the station on departure.

Reward tickets (i.e. those obtained using your EurostarÂ Frequent Traveller membership points) may be collected either by the member or by those named on the tickets - again, subject to the booking reference and some form of identification being produced at the time of collection.

It is essential that you retain your booking reference for clarification in the event of queries.

Travelling from Brussels, Lille or Calais to the UK

The UK authorities are carrying out additional document checks on arrival in the UK from Brussels, Lille and Calais. If you are travelling from any of these destinations, please allow an extra 30 minutes after your arrival time in your travel plans. On three services - the 9133, 9149 and 9161 - an additional ticket check will take place at your seat during the journey.

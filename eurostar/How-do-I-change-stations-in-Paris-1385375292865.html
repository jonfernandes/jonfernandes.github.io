How do I change stations in Paris?

Below you’ll find information on how to change trains in Paris. - For stations in the south of France, you’ll need to change to Gare de Lyon - For stations in the east of France, you’ll need to change to Gare de l’Est - For stations in the west of France, you’ll need to change to Gare Montparnasse - For stations in the north of France, you’ll need to change to Gare de Saint Lazare - For stations in central France, you’ll need to change to Gare de Bercy - How to change for Gare d’Austerlitz

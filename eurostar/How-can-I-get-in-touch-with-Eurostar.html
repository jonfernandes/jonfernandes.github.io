How can I get in touch with Eurostar?

Bookings, exchanges & cancellations

  • Book online (no service fee)

  • Manage your booking online:

- download your ticket
- upgrade*
- change your travel date or time*
- select your seat (journeys to/from London only)
- select your meal preference if you have a Business Premier or Standard Premier ticket (journeys to/from London only)
- view your options if your train is cancelled

*You may have to pay exchange fees and/or the difference in price between your old and your new ticket. Please refer to your ticket after-sales conditions. Find out more


Club Eurostar
Log into your Club Eurostar account to:
- Claim, redeem or share points,
- View your points statement,
- Update your account details.


Still need help?
The best way to contact us is via our Contact Us form. This allows us to prioritise queries and respond as quickly and efficiently as possible. If your query is about an existing booking, please provide your booking reference.

Or call us on: +44 (0)3432 186 186 (cost of a local call depending on the operator). A £12 / 14 € / $20 service fee may apply.

Our customer service team is available: Monday to Sunday - 07:00 - 19:00 (UK time)


Traveller Care
To let us know how your trip went, get in touch with our Traveller Care team. The best way to contact us is via our Contact Us form where you can provide all details about your enquiry, including attachments where applicable. This allows us to prioritise and investigate queries more efficiently.

If you’ve submitted a claim to our Traveller Care team but are unsatisfied with the response, or if you haven’t received any response within three months, you may wish to contact an alternative dispute resolution service.

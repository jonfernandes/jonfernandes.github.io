e320 - What other changes are being made in the stations?

The e320 is slightly longer than the TMST and e300 and the stopping point for all trains in our stations has been reviewed. We’re making a few changes at platform level, like moving some rain canopy pillars and security huts in Paris, before the e320s start running. 

We launched our station capacity upgrade plan two years ago and it’s going really well. 

We’re installing ePassport gates at St Pancras in late 2015 to increase the capacity at the French border control (PAF). This follows the installation of new security screening earlier this year.

The check-in area in Brussels-Midi is being expanded and will soon be able to handle 900 travellers per hour. ePassport gates will also be installed.

Paris Nord will see a 40% increase in capacity by late 2016. Check-in will be extended, ePassport gates will be installed, and the Business Premier lounge will be moved to create a bigger main lounge and a great new Business Premier lounge.

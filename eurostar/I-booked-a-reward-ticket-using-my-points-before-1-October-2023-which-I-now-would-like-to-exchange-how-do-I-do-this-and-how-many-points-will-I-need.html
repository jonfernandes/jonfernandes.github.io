I booked a reward ticket using my points before 1 October 2023 which I now would like to exchange, how do I do this and how many points will I need?

Any reward tickets purchased before 1 October 2023 for travel to/from London can be exchanged once within the same class of service without requiring any additional points to be redeemed. They cannot be refunded.

All other reward tickets purchased before 1st October 2023 can be cancelled and refunded (Comfort, Premium ONLY) at the original points value prior to departure. They cannot be exchanged.

You will only be able to exchange/refund tickets through any of our Contact Centres for no fee.

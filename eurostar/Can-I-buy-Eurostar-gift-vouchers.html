Can I buy Eurostar gift vouchers?

Yes. You can buy  giftvouchers from £25 to £300 at vouchers.eurostar.com to email to friends and family.
 
Just choose an amount and a design, write a message and tell us when you'd like to send it, then we'll take care of the rest.
 
Eurostar gift vouchers are valid for one year from the day you buy them and can only be used to book tickets on eurostar.com. They can be used alongside other vouchers and there's no need to spend them all in one go. If the full amount isn't used, it'll just stay on the gift voucher to be used before the expiry date.

Car parking restrictions at Ebbsfleet International Terminal during the Olympic Games. (25th July 2012 – 10th September 2012)

You are strongly advised to pre-book your car parking at Ebbsfleet International during the Olympic Games period from 25th July 2012 until 10th September 2012 included with www.ncp.co.uk Customers who have pre-booked via www.ncp.co.uk will be able to park in Car Park B, a short distance from the Terminal. There are car parking spaces available in Car Park B for blue disabled badge holders. Available car parking spaces will be extremely limited if you turn up on the day without having pre-booked. These available spaces will be approximately 10 minutes’ walk away from the terminal.

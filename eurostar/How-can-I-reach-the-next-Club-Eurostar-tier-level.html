How can I reach the next Club Eurostar tier level?

It’s all about the Status points accumulated within your membership year.

The more you travel, the more points you’ll gain. And the more points you gain, the higher your tier.

**To achieve Avantage, earn 500 Status points (within your membership year)**

**To achieve Carte Blanche, earn 2,900 Status points**

**To achieve Etoile, earn over 5000 Status points**

When you have enough points you’ll be automatically upgraded into the next tier. You then retain that tier for the remainder of your membership year and the following year.

Each tier has numerous benefits and bonuses. And as you climb the tiers, those perks just keep adding up.

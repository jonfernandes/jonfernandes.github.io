I'm in Carte Blanche and want to go into the Business Premier lounge - what do I need to bring with me?

You must have a valid ticket and Carte Blanche membership card, both in your name: you’ll be asked to show them when you enter the lounge. If your membership has expired or you don’t have a valid membership card, you won’t be able to access the lounge.

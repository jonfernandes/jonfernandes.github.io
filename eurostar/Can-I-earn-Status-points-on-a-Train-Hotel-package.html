Can I earn Status points on a Train + Hotel package?

You can! For every €2 or $2 spent, you’ll earn 1 Status point. And for every £2 spent, you’ll earn 1.2 Status points.

Status points are only awarded to the lead passenger on a Train + Hotel booking. And only one membership number can be linked to each booking. Other travellers on the booking will not be entitled to earn points.

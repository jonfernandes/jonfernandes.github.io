What are Status points and how do I earn them?

As a Club Eurostar member, you’ll earn Status points for tickets purchased across Eurostar services once you’ve travelled.

For every €1 or $1 spent on train tickets, you’ll earn 1 Status point. And for every £1 spent, you’ll earn 1.2 Status points.

Status points decide which membership level you’re in. The more Status points you have, the higher you’ll be. You’ll need a certain amount each membership year to maintain your level or upgrade to the next level.

Watch out— Status points reset to 0 at the end of your membership year, so if you want to maintain your level, make sure you have enough points before the date stated on your account.

How can I pay for my Eurostar ticket?

You’ll need to pay for your ticket when you book, so have a debit or credit card to hand, as well as any e-vouchers* you’d like to spend. 
 

We currently accept:
  • MasterCard
  • Visa
  • American Express
  • Maestro
  • Apple Pay
  • Cartes bancaires (French website)
  • Bancontact (Belgian website)
  • PayPal and Pay in 3 (for train only bookings on UK, US, German, Belgian and Rest of the World websites)
With respect to online banking, bookers in the Netherlands can use iDEAL and bookers in Germany can use Sofort.

If you’re collecting your tickets at the station, don’t forget to bring your payment card with you.

Please note that a service fee of £12/€14 per call or transaction at the station applies to Standard and Standard Premier bookings and exchanges.

We can only accept British pounds at UK stations and Euros at Continental stations.
Cash is not accepted at London St Pancras.

*e-vouchers are only accepted for booking made on Eurostar.com

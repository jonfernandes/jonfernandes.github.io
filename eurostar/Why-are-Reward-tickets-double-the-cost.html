Why are Reward tickets double the cost?

In re-aligning the two programmes into one we have had to review the number of points it costs to redeem a free ticket to make it fair for all our members.

Members of Club Eurostar before 1st October have received 20% bonus points at launch of the new programme. On top of this you can receive an additional 25%-75% bonus points on each booking depending on your club level. Visit Eurostar.com for further details.

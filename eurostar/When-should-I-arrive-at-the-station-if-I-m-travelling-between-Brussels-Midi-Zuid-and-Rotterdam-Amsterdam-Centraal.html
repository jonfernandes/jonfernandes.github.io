When should I arrive at the station if I’m travelling between Brussels-Midi/Zuid and Rotterdam/Amsterdam Centraal?

Station staff and police officers may carry out random security checks before you board at Brussels-Midi/Zuid. So, please arrive at least 20 minutes before departure to allow time for this.


Alcohol on Board:
Although you can bring alcohol with you and we serve alcohol on board, safety is our top priority. So, please drink in moderation.
If you behave in an antisocial way which ruins the journey for other passengers or break any laws or by-laws, we might ask you to leave the train at the nearest station.

How will I get my tickets?

Whatever your setup, there are three options to choose from.

Print your tickets at home or save them on your phone. To select either option, log in and manage your booking using your six-letter booking reference and your last name. From here, you'll be able to select how you receive your ticket and travel information.

Go to manage a booking 

Or find your tickets in the Eurostar app and use your six-letter booking reference and your last name.

Download the Eurostar app

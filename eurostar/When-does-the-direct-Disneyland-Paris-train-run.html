When does the direct Disneyland® Paris train run?

We run direct trains to Disneyland® Paris at popular times like school holidays and UK bank holiday weekends.
 
There are usually five trains a week during the summer holidays and four a week in term time, except in January and February when the direct train doesn’t run.
 
The rest of the time, you can take a Eurostar train to Lille, from where it’s just a simple switch of platforms to carry on to the Magic Kingdom. Whichever route you choose, you can book it all in one go at eurostar.com.

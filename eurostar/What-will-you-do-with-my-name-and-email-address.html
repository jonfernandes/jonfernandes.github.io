What will you do with my name and email address?


You’ll need to enter your name and email address to log into our wi-fi. It’s up to you whether you sign up to receive news and offers from Eurostar though. If you’d rather not, just don’t tick the box – you’ll still be able to use the wi-fi. To find out more, see our Privacy Policy.

Can I get a refund instead of an e-voucher?

We offer compensation for trains that are delayed over 60 minutes, so depending on how long you were delayed, you may be able to claim a Eurostar e-voucher.
More information on our compensation e-vouchers.
 
If you’d prefer, we can offer you a partial refund instead of an e-voucher. You need to claim a refund within 60 days of the disruption. Here’s what you’ll get:

Length of delay

Refund amount

60-119 minutes

25% of the value of the affected ticket

120 minutes or more

50% of the value of the affected ticket

Refunds will be returned within 14 days of us receiving your claim.

Please use Google Chrome to claim your compensation. All you’ll need is your booking reference and ticket number.

Or copy these links:
To claim your e-voucher please go to https://compensation.eurostar.com/?lang=EN#/
To claim a refund please go to https://prr.eurostar.com/?lang=en#/

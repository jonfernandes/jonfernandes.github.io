How do I make a booking using Eco Cheques?

Eurostar is participating in the Eco Pass/Ecochèques scheme; however it is not possible to use these vouchers to purchase Eurostar tickets directly with us. To exchange your Eco Pass/Ecochèques for Eurostar tickets please go to any of the Jetair centres in Belgium. For more information on this please visit their web site at www.jetaircenter.be/agences.htm

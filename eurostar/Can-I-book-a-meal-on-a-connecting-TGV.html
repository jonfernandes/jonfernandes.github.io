Can I book a meal on a connecting train (e.g. TGV)?

For travel until 3 November
We can only guarantee you a meal on the Eurostar part of your journey (if you’re travelling in Standard Premier or Business Premier). 

For travel on or after 4 November
We can only guarantee you a meal on the Eurostar part of your journey (if you’re travelling in Eurostar Plus on London routes or in Eurostar Premier on all routes).

You can buy food on board some European trains, otherwise there are plenty of shops at our stations where you can pick up a snack or two to keep you going.

I have just made a seat assignment for a booking on my GDS, it shows the seat number as 000 and the message SSR OTHS 1A 2385 SEGMENT/S OVERBOOKED REQUEST SEAT/S AT CHECK-IN what does this mean?

This means that the client has been allocated an "overbooked seat" as there are no seats available. Please offer an alternative service where possible.

I booked my journey before the launch of the new Club Eurostar programme, but I am travelling after, how many points will I earn?

Reward and status points are earned based on travel date so you will earn points based on the new earning rates below:

TRAIN ONLY
1 point for every €1 spent
1 point for every $1 spent
1.2 points for every £1 spent

TRAIN & HOTEL
1 point for every €2 spent
1 point for every $2 spent
1.2 points for every £2 spent

What are the fare conditions between Brussels and Netherlands?

Tickets are exchangeable for free up to departure; you’ll need to pay any difference in fare per person and per leg of journey.
If the fare available at the time of the exchange is less expensive than the original fare purchased, the difference in price will not be reimbursed.

A £10 service fee per transaction will be charged for Standard and Standard Premier booking changes made over the phone.

Changes to your journey must be made before the planned departure of your original train.

All tickets are non-refundable.


Please note that a €5 service fee is charged for printing tickets in the ticket offices in the Netherlands.

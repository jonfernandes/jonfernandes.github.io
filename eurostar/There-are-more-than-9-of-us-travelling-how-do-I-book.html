There are more than 9 of us travelling, how do I book?

If there are more than 9 people travelling in your group please contact our Groups team on ​+44 (0)3448 224 800 or alternatively you can get a quote via our dedicated page.

Open from 8am to 6pm Monday to Friday. Closed at weekends & Bank Holidays

 



Alcohol on Board:
Although you can bring alcohol with you and we serve alcohol on board, safety is our top priority. So, please drink in moderation.
If you behave in an antisocial way which ruins the journey for other passengers or break any laws or by-laws, we might ask you to leave the train at the nearest station.

Why did the Business Premier lounge move in Paris Gare du Nord?

Our new Business Premier lounge is part of our wider expansion of the Eurostar area at Gare du Nord.

Now the Business Premier lounge is open, we’ll start transforming the main Eurostar departure lounge to welcome 20% more travellers, improve the flow and make it much more comfortable for everyone by end 2017.
 
We know that our old Business Premier lounge was a bit tricky to get to. That’s why our new location is much easier and faster to get to – using the lifts or stairs directly after check-in – giving you more time to relax before boarding your train. It also has a fresh new look, designed around you, to make sure it’s the perfect place to relax and unwind or catch up on work before you board.

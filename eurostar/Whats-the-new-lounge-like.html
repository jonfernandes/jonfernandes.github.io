What's the new lounge like?

We’ve designed our brand new lounge around you, to make sure it’s the perfect place to relax and unwind or catch up on work before you board. Taking inspiration from a chic Parisian apartment, it’s light, bright and spacious, with lots of original 19th-century features, and specially commissioned works of art. With an elegant cocktail bar and comfy sofas, cosy nooks for peace and quiet, and work spaces with wireless chargers, there’s a space to suit every mood. We’ve also taken your feedback on board and we’re planning regular events and experiences around the bar, as well as exhibitions showcasing work from up-and-coming local artists.

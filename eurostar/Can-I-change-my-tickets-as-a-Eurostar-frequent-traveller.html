Can I change my tickets as a Eurostar frequent traveller?

Tickets purchased using points from a Eurostar carte blanche account can be exchanged as many times as required before departure, subject to availability on the chosen service.


Tickets purchased using points from a Eurostar carte classique account can be exchanged once only before departure, subject to availability on the chosen service.

Tickets purchased from Eurostar's public fare range, without using points to pay for them, are subject to their own terms and conditions.


Eurostar™ Frequent Traveller membership does not allow for any exception to be made to these conditions.

I’ve bought a ticket to Brussels-Midi/Zuid – can I use it to travel to another station in the city?

On our trains to and from London, on the same day as your journey, you can use your ticket to travel by over-ground train between Brussels-Midi/Zuid and Stations in Zone Brussels:

  • Bockstael

  • Boondaal

  • Bordet

  • Bosvoorde

  • Brussel-Centraal

  • Brussel-Congres

  • Brussel-Kapellekerk

  • Brussel-Luxembourg

  • Brussel-Noord

  • Brussel-Schuman

  • Brussel-West

  • Brussels-Midi/Zuid

  • Delta

  • Diesdelle

  • Etterbeek

  • Evere

  • Haren

  • Haren-Zuid

  • Jette

  • Meiser

  • Merode

  • Moensberg

  • Schaarbeek

  • Simonis

  • Sint-Agatha-Berchem

  • St.-Job

  • Ukkel-Kalevoet

  • Ukkel-Stalle

  • Vorst-Oost

  • Vorst-Zuid

  • Watermaal

 
You can’t use your ticket to travel by tram, on the metro or to the airport.

If you have to catch a connecting train in the main terminal building, please give yourself at least an hour to find the correct platform.

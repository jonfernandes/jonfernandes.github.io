How do I book a FIP or PRIV ticket?

If you’re a current Eurostar colleague

You can book FIP or PRIV fares on our internal booking app or in the ticket office at St Pancras International.

 
If you don’t work for Eurostar or are a retired Eurostar employee
 

If you are a current or retired UK rail company employee, you can visit the International Rail website at www.bookmyrst.co.uk or contact Trains Europe by email at sales@trainseurope.co.uk or by phone on +44 1354 660222 or +44 1354 705098.
Otherwise, you should speak to your HR or staff travel department. If your HR or staff travel department cannot provide assistance, you should contact Trains Europe by email at sales@trainseurope.co.uk or by phone on +44 1354 660222 or +44 1354 705098.

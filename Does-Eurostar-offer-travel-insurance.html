Does Eurostar offer travel insurance?

 
Travelling between Belgium, France, the Netherlands and Germany

On these routes, cancellation insurance can be purchased indirectly through distribution partners.
Your ticket distributor is indicated on your confirmation of purchase. Contact them directly for a cancellation or insurance policy.

Travelling to and from London

For your trips to and from London you can buy travel insurance, which includes cover for cancellation.
To make it simple, we have added the option to select insurance during the booking process. You can also add it retrospectively, once you’ve received your booking confirmation via email.
If you can’t commit right now, take your time and get a quote whenever suits you from our trusted provider, Allianz Global.

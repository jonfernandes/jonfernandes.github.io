How do I book an accessible hotel with Expedia?

First, go to the Hotels tab on eurostar.com and tap in where you’re going and when, and how many rooms you’d like to book.

On the results page, you’ll see Filter hotels by on the left-hand side. Simply scroll down to find the Accessibility options and choose how you’d like to filter the results. The results will then update to show hotels that offer your chosen feature.
 
Once you’ve chosen a hotel, you’ll need to add details of your request to the Special/Accessibility requests section on the checkout page. Just so you know, Expedia will pass on your request to the hotel but they can’t guarantee it will be met.
 
If the hotel is able to meet your request you should get a confirmation email within 24 hours. If you don’t, please contact the hotel direct to see what assistance they can provide.
